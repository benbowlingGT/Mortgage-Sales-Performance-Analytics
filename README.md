# Mortgage Loan Officer Performance Dashboard

##  Business Problem
Mortgage sales leadership lacked a centralized view of pipeline health and individual officer momentum. High-level volume was being tracked, but the business couldn't easily identify "leakage" in the conversion funnel or distinguish between high-volume veterans and high-growth "rising stars."

##  The Solution
I developed a 3-page interactive Power BI suite that transforms raw loan application data (100k+ rows) into actionable sales intelligence.

### Key Insights:
* **Funnel Leakage:** Identified a **19.3% withdrawal rate** (nearly 1 in 5 apps), highlighting a major opportunity for improved lead qualification.
* **Momentum vs. Volume:** While high-volume officers manage the bulk of the **$829M pipeline**, trend analysis identified **Monica F.** as the "Rising Star" with **30.2% YoY growth**.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Tracked an average **39.2 days to close**, providing a benchmark for regional performance comparisons.

## 🛠️Technical Stack
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop / Service
* **Data Prep:** Power Query (M) for data cleaning and normalization.
* **Modeling:** DAX for YoY growth calculations, Pull-Through rates, and dynamic currency formatting ($M/$K).
* **UI/UX:** Executive-style layout with a global filter rail and synced slicers.
